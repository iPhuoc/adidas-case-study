Intro

This is a short Overview about my Case Study at Adidas

Task

One of the main tasks within the application process at Adidas is the Case Study.

This serves to prove the applicants expertise in Statistics, Machine Learning and AI.
In Addition to that, the applicants also have to show their coding style and their ability to relate to the business they applied for.

Data

First of all the data are NOT real data from the business. Adidas provided two datasets(sales.txt and article_attributes.txt).

sales.txt containes transactional data of adidas stores containing historical sales data per article, week and country.
article_attributes.txt contains the attribute data of the sold articles.

Approach

After importing & cleaning the data I did some exploratory Analysis and Data Visualisation. I generated some Insights about the Products, Marketing Campaigns and Sales. After that I built a Model which predicts for each product the revenue for the following year.
